VACCINES AVAILABLE IN INDIA

1.COVISHIELD

The Covid-19 vaccine was developed by Oxford University in partnership with British-Swedish firm AstraZeneca. It is being manufactured in India by Pune-based Serum Institute of India (SII).

2.COVAXIN

Covaxin is an indigenous vaccine developed by Hyderabad-based Bharat Biotech International Limited in collaboration with the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) and National Institute of Virology, Pune (NIV).
